The Medium

Our Materials

Every Erfolg Living piece begins as raw metal — chosen for its character, then raised, hammered and finished entirely by hand. Scroll to meet the five metals at the heart of our craft.

01

Cu · 29

Copper

Hand-raised and ever-changing — copper develops a living patina that deepens with every passing year, the warmest and most characterful of metals.

02

Fe + Cr

Stainless Steel

The modern edge — architectural, cool to the touch and utterly incorruptible. Clean lines that hold their brilliance for life.

03

Cu + Zn

Brass

Moradabad's signature alloy — warm, golden and quietly opulent. The very metal our city, India's Brass City, is named for.

04

Sn · 50

Tin

Soft-bright and food-safe — the time-honoured lining of fine copperware, glowing a gentle, understated silver.

05

Ni · 28

Nickel

A cool, mirror-bright finish — silver-white, resilient and quietly modern. The jeweller's lustre on architectural metal.
