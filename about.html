Our Story

Three decades of traditional coppersmithing mastery, passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

A 30-Year Heritage

Founded on Tradition,
Polished by Innovation

Founded in 1994, Erfolg Living began as a small family-owned smithy dedicated to the ancient art of hand-hammered metalwork. What started as a passion for authentic materials has evolved into a global benchmark for luxury copperware.

Every bathtub, sink, and fixture we produce is a one-of-a-kind masterpiece. We don't just manufacture; we compose stories in metal that age gracefully alongside the homes they inhabit.

Our Core Philosophy

Beyond aesthetics, our work is guided by three fundamental commitments.

Sustainability

We use 100% recyclable, lead-free copper. Our pieces are built to last lifetimes, reducing the environmental footprint of modern luxury.

Artisan DNA

No machines. No assembly lines. Just the rhythmic sound of the hammer meeting the metal, guided by eyes that see excellence.

Wellness

Copper is naturally antimicrobial and offers superior heat retention, turning your daily routine into a rejuvenating ritual.
